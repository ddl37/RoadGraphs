Intro

Note: So now we want to apply this to create a solution on real-world roads (that is, a placement of sensors on edges of the graph) - we decided to use Auckland. Introducing real-world road conditions makes the problem significantly more challenging. Earlier we gave conditions for obtaining *complete* information. However, that may be too many sensors for NZTA to consider, so we want to scale down the number of sensors while preserving as much information as possible.
